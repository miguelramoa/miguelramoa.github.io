GREEN MASK

Once upon a time, there was a young hero who was plagued by anxiety. He couldn't make a decision, even if it was something as simple as what to eat for breakfast. But one day, he stumbled upon a green mask floating in the air. As soon as he put the mask on, he felt a strange peace wash over him. He was no longer afraid to make decisions and he felt confident in himself. The hero quickly realized that this mask represented his newfound calm and he wore it proudly as he continued on his journey./p>

A young hero was overwhelmed by depression and felt like giving up on his journey. But then, he stumbled upon a blue mask floating in the air. As soon as he put the mask on, he felt a strange hope wash over him. He was no longer consumed by his negative thoughts and instead felt empowered to keep going. The hero quickly realized that this mask represented his newfound hope and he wore it proudly as he continued on his journey.

RED MASK

A young hero was consumed by anger. He was constantly lashing out at those around him and couldn't control his emotions. That was until he found the red mask. As soon as he put the mask on, he felt a strange power surge through him. He no longer felt the need to lash out and instead, he channeled his anger into defeating the enemies that stood in his way. The hero soon realized that this mask represented his newfound control and he wore it proudly as he continued on his journey.
